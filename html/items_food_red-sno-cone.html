Red Sno Cone

Category: Food

A tongue-tingling spine-shiveringly Red Sno Cone. A seamless blend of Ice, Hooch, and a secret blend of elemental additives, preservatives, iMG and raw energy that make you feel slightly, though not to any copyright-infringing extent, like you may have wings.

  • This can be made with a Blender or purchased from a Sno Cone Vendor.
  • Worth about 600 currants
  • Fits 10 in a backpack slot
